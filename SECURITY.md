# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

At Scanago, we take security seriously. If you discover any security issues or vulnerabilities in our application, please let us know by contacting us via email at [omrajpal.exe@gmail.com](mailto:omrajpal.exe@gmail.com).

When reporting a vulnerability, please provide detailed information, including:

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce the vulnerability
- Potential impact of the vulnerability

Our team will acknowledge your report promptly, investigate the issue, and take necessary actions to address it. We appreciate your contribution to keeping Scanago secure.

## Responsible Disclosure

We encourage responsible disclosure and adhere to a coordinated disclosure process. This means:

- Please allow us a reasonable amount of time to investigate and address the issue before disclosing it publicly.
- We commit to keeping you informed about the progress of resolving the vulnerability.

Thank you for helping us improve the security of Scanago.
